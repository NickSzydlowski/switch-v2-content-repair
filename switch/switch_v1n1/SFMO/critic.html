Speed and impediment seem in sum to be the polarities of the critical stance; speed of attention followed by impediment. The capacity to see far ahead and the problematic pace in zooming in on the objective... As is well known, art is not a clear territory defined by a meaning which blocks its borders; it has a dense friability which renders it's contours positively uncertain. The only criticism possible is one which does not believe in the dogma of art, in that it accepts with much stoicism, cynicism and hedonism the infathomable way to truth. Fortunately.
