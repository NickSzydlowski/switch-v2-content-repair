Antisituationsists are formalists at heart, but they would never admit to that. Not only are they formalists but they also hate any art that challenges their notions of what is art. They tend to be boring and are members of the group: Believe everything you see. They like two kinds of art which actually refers back to them: predictable art and boring art. They hate the idea of surprise, shock, and chance in art and most of all they hate the idea of ideas. They are famous for their lack of thought or insight. They are the same people who will look at Richter's work and only see his expert paint handling and nothing else.
