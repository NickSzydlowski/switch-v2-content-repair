MER


Formed in 1985 by Dr. J.C. Withers, president, and Dr. R.O. Loutfy, CEO. They are both principal investigators of project in the field of Fullerene technology sponsored by NASA, the U.S. Army, and other companies. The company employees a team of about 35 researchers for technological development and production of Fullerenes. Their primary goals are the production of Fullerenes, technology development and applications of Fullerenes.
