Eternal Youth


Based on the principle that the first building block is the molecule, Drexler's assemblers would be able to build virtually "anything that was physically possible, automatically, at nearly zero cost, thereby eradicating poverty, while at the same time curing all known illnesses making people physically younger, enabling them to live for hundreds, perhaps even thousands of years." They would relieve humanity of poverty, disease and aging. Material goods are one thing, but dealing with the physiology of the human body, a living organism, is another. This concept was explored in a recent Outer Limits episode. The scope of what Drexler sees ahead is a disease-free world, where lifetimes are extended and where the "complete control of the structure of matter...(is) complete control of human biology." He also imagines the potentially negative effects of this technology if it got into the wrong hands.
