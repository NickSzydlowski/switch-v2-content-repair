Ih (Icosahedron)


Icosahedron: One of the five platonic solids which has 20 faces (equilateral triangles), 12 vertices and 30 edges. Schoenfies symbols are I- for an icosahedron with order 60 symmetrical operations and Ih - for an icosahedron with order 120 symmetrical operations including reflections and inversions. Truncation of vertices produces C60 geometry.
