Politics of VR

BL: And when I say 'politics,' I mean that there's something very important politically about preserving the integrity of the physical body, of the sensory body, of the self as embodied creature with a single point of view that moves around in the world. It's all well and good to have instrumentalities like weather satellites giving us looks at the world, but a medium that strives to dissolve our sense of being embodied is fraught with a great deal of philosophical difficulties for people who are concerned with our progressive alienation from nature, and it caters to some dangerous fantasies.

Any medium that further weakens my sense of my physical existence, right now, in real time, weakens my political power and my political will to take care of myself. And it weakens my ability to take care of the planet -- to understand what life is.
